I. Who are Talent Agency ?

Talent Agency was created by Mathilde Ganigal, an artistic agent.
Mathilde Ganigal, a young ESSEC graduate, combines marketing and financial experience with an extensive network in the audiovisual and film sector.
Always on the lookout for new talent, Talent Agency provides experienced actors, actresses, directors and writers to professionals in the audiovisual, theatre and film industries.
Talent Agency manages the image of its talents.
Talent Agency currently represents 95 talents, including a children's and teenagers' section.
